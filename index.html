About Omnitrade Manpower Solutions Ltd.

Founded on the principles of reliability and corporate excellence, **Omnitrade Manpower Solutions Ltd.** is a premier human capital and facility management agency based in Surrey, British Columbia. We specialize in providing the essential workforce that keeps the Canadian economy moving—from high-velocity logistics centers to luxury residential hospitality.

We established Omnitrade to bridge the gap between large-scale enterprise needs and local execution. We don't just provide labor; we provide strategic partners. Whether we are managing a 24-hour warehouse operation, facilitating seamless Airbnb turnovers, or maintaining strata complexes, our standard remains "Precision and Performance."

With a deep understanding of the BC landscape, Omnitrade is committed to safety, efficiency, and the growth of our people. We serve as a vital link in the supply chain, ensuring that every delivery, every room, and every facility reflects the standard our clients deserve.
