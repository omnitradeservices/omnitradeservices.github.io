About Omnitrade

Omnitrade Manpower Solutions Ltd. is a premier 3rd-party service provider based in Surrey, BC. We specialize in bridging the gap between large-scale enterprise needs and local execution through high-standard staffing and facility management.

From managing high-velocity logistics centers to maintaining luxury residential properties and strata complexes, our team is committed to precision and corporate integrity. We serve as a vital link in the supply chain, ensuring that every facility we touch reflects a multi-million dollar standard of excellence.
